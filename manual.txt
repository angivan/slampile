SLAM-PILE should be executed in a Linux enviroment, and it is dependent on following packages or softwares: 
R, Python 3, Rsamtools (R language), pandas (python language), samtools.

To use SLAM-PILE, please change following filenames at the very top of "run_slampile.py" to your real filenames.
'''INPUTs'''
bam_filename = 'e.g. x.bam'
gtf_filename = 'e.g. hg38_transcriptome.gtf'
fa_filename =  'e.g. hg38.fa'
